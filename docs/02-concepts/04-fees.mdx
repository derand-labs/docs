# Fees

## Request Fee

Each Profile defines its own fee model:

```text
requestFee = baseFee + delay * delayFee
```

## Callback Execution Fee

- Are charged separately from the request fee.
- Depend on the actual gas cost at execution time.
- If the requester does not have sufficient balance, the callback is skipped without affecting the randomness request itself.
- If the proof submitter does not provide sufficient gas or callback funding, the entire proof submission transaction reverts.

## Collateral

Given a request with fee <RequestFeeDetail text="R"/>.

When the request is created, the requester must deposit native coins equal to <RequestFeeShortHand n="1"/>.

When the request is assigned, the selected prover must provide collateral equal to <RequestFeeShortHand n="106"/>.

The collateral amount is chosen to ensure that all possible penalties can be fully covered.

The maximum possible penalties are:

- Up to <RequestFeeShortHand n="1"/> for failing to submit preview randomness on time.
- Up to <RequestFeeShortHand n="1"/> for failing to submit the final proof on time.
- Up to <RequestFeeShortHand n="5"/> for [open-completion rewards and penalties](#open-state).
- <RequestFeeShortHand n="100"/> for submitting preview randomness that differs from the final verified randomness.

Therefore, the maximum possible penalty is <RequestFeeShortHand n="107"/> (equal to the total collateral provided by the requester and prover).

Collateral is released once a valid proof has been successfully submitted.

## Rewards, Penalties, and Compensation

For each assigned request, the prover must do the following to avoid being slashed:

- Submit preview randomness within `20 * delayTime * delay` seconds.
- Submit the final proof within:
  - `20 * delayTime * delay + 1.5 * baseTime` seconds after assignment, or
  - `1.5 * baseTime` seconds after submitting preview randomness.

Violations result in the following penalties:

Violation                                                     | Penalty
------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------
Late preview randomness                                       | Up to <RequestFeeShortHand n="1"/>
Late final proof                                              | Up to <RequestFeeShortHand n="1"/>
Preview randomness differs from the final verified randomness | <RequestFeeShortHand n="100"/>

### Open state

If no valid proof is submitted within:

- `20 * delayTime * delay + 3 * baseTime` after assignment, or
- `3 * baseTime` after submitting preview randomness,

the request enters the **Open** state.

In the **Open** state:

- Anyone may submit a valid proof.
- The first successful submitter receives a reward between <RequestFeeShortHand n="1"/> and <RequestFeeShortHand n="3"/> (earlier submissions receive larger rewards).
- The original prover receives an additional penalty between 0 and <RequestFeeShortHand n="4"/> (earlier completion results in a smaller penalty).

The combined Open-state reward and penalty is always between <RequestFeeShortHand n="3"/> and <RequestFeeShortHand n="5"/>.

## Collateral Distribution

- All penalties and rewards are paid from the locked collateral.
- Penalties compensate the requester.
- Open-completion rewards are paid to the first participant who successfully submits a valid proof.
- The [Treasury Budget Fee](/docs/07-treasury.md) is collected, up to the remaining available collateral.
- Any remaining collateral is returned to the assigned prover.
